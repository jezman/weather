LCD Screen MT-20S4A 20x4 (Made in Russia). Based on HD44780.

| LCD Pin| Pin assignment | Raspberry Pi   |
|:---:|:-----------------:|:--------------:|
| 1  | GND                |            GND |
| 2  | +3V/5V             |           +5V  |
| 3  | Uo                 | GPIO 23/Pin 16 |
| 4  | Ao                 | GPIO 7/Pin 26  |
| 5  | R/W                |            GND |
| 6  | E                  |  GPIO 8/Pin 24 |
| 11 | DB4                | GPIO 18/Pin 12 |
| 12 | DB5                | GPIO 17/Pin 11 |
| 13 | DB6                | GPIO 27/Pin 14 |
| 14 | DB7                | GPIO 22/Pin 15 |
| 15 | +LED               |           +5V  |
| 16 | -LED               |           GND  |

* Pin 7-10 not used.
* GPIO 16 - Ralay RTD14005.
